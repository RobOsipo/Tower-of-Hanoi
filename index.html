Tower of Hanoi is a puzzle that involves three rods and a number of disks. Seen above are three rods, and at default, three disks. The goal of the puzzle is to move the stack of disks to the third rod, with some rules. You must move the disks one at a time, and at no point can you place a larger disk on top of a smaller disk. With three disks, this is possible with a sequence of seven moves, but the puzzle can resolved with any number of disks.

To interact with the puzzle above, click within the space of the rod that has the disk you would like to move, and then click within the space of the rod you would like to move it to. Each time you do this, your move will be counted, and you can compare the number of moves you have made to the minimum number of moves required to solve the puzzle, the goal.
